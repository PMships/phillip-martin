Irish Newspaper Archives
Director, Operations & Commercial
2003 — Present
Active

Joined shortly after the company's Enterprise Ireland HPSU designation. Helped build it from a microfilm-conversion service into the world's largest commercial Irish newspaper archive, with a digital platform live since 2009.

Owned product, institutional B2B/B2G sales, rights and licensing, and earned-media positioning. Continually expanded the corpus across national and regional press — most recently the Irish News, Belfast Telegraph, Mayo Examiner, and The Corkman.
